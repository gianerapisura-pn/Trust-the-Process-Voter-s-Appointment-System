ELIGIBILITY TO VOTE

  • Citizenship – Must be a Filipino citizen. Natural-born or naturalized. Filipinos who reacquire citizenship under R.A. 9225 (Dual Citizens) may also register.
  • Age – Must be at least 18 years old on or before Election Day.
  • Residency – Must have resided in the Philippines for at least one (1) year immediately preceding the election. Must have resided in the place (city, municipality, or barangay) where they intend to vote for at least six (6) months before the election.
